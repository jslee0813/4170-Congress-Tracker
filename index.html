Help

How to search,

Click on the Search link or type the shortcut, Alt + S, to launch the Search dialog. Pick the State, Chamber, and District that you want to search for the congress members. Click on the Search button or type the shortcut, Alt + S. The system will find the congress memebers for your search criteria and show the results on the result page. Thd District field is only required for House of Representative. On the Result pages, two Senators for the State would be rendered and one House of Representative for the State and Distrit would be rendered. The color of the border will be red or blue based on the member's role. The color of red represents the republican and the color of red represents the democrat. If the homepage, twitter accounts, facebook accounts, or youtube accounts are available for a congress member, the corresponding icons would be shown, so the user can navigate to the extra pages.
