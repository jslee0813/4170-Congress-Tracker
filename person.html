Help

Search for a Member of Congress:

Click on the Search link at the top right of the screen (shortcut: Alt + S), to launch the Search dialog. Pick the State and Chamber from the drop down menu. If looking for a member of the House of Representatives, choose the District from the drop down menu. Click on the Search button (shortcut: Alt + S). If looking for a member of the Senate, you will then be asked to choose one of the two Senators from the chosen state. The system will find the congress member that satisfies your search criteria and show the results on a new page. The color scheme of this page corresponds to the party of the Member of Congress: red for Republican, and blue for Democrat.

Find a Member of Congress's voting record:

Navigate to the page of the Member of Congress whose voting record you wish to see (Search for a Member of Congress). On the right side of the results page is a colored box labelled Voting Record. Click on the button with the blue triangle () that is to the left of this label to show the voting record and number of bills for the Member of Congress. These statistics are broken down by the session of Congress. For example, the label specifies the voting and bill information for the Member of Congress during the 113th United States Congress in the House of Representatives. To minimize this information, click on the button with the blue triangle () that is to the left of the label Voting Record.


Find informatation about a Member of Congress's campaign finances:

Search for the Congressman or Senator you wish to see (Search for a Member of Congress). Under the individual's biography, you will find financial data about this member of Congress' campaign finances during his or her last campaign. This includes data such as total contributions, disbursements, and receipts from this Congress member's last campaign for election.
