Help

What Can I Do?

Search for a Congressman or Senator to:

  1. View their biography
  2. Check out the finances from their last campaign
  3. See their voting record
    1. Voting records are displayed by year, e.g. 112th United States Congress in the House of Representatives
  4. Get a list of recent legislative bills introduced
  5. Read about them in the New York Times Paper

How Can I Do It?

How to search for a Congressman or Senator:

  1. Click the Search button in the top right corner of the navigation bar. You'll get a popup.
  2. Select a state from the "State" dropdown
  3. Decide which chamber you'd like to view by choosing either "Senate" or "House" under the "Chamber" dropdown
  4. If you chose "House" as the Chamber, you'll get a new popdown bar which will let you pick which specific Congressional District you'd like to view.
  5. If you chose "Senate" as the Chamber, you'll also get a new popdown bar which lets you select which of the 2 State Senators you'd lke to view.
  6. Hit the "Search" button on the bottom of the pop-up and you're ready to go!

Can I Favorite a Congress Member?

Yes! Every Congressman and Senator's page has a star located next to their name. Click the star to mark them as a "Favorite" and click again to "Unfavorite". Any favorited members of Congress will then be located under the Favorites dropdown in the navigation bar. Click any of the names in the dropdown for direct access to the person's homepage. Every time you login, your homepage will be tailored to the Congress members you've favorited. We'll have the most recent articles related to them in your homepage's News Feed.

Are there any shortcuts?

Of course! Hit Alt-S on your keyboard and you'll get the search box popup ASAP. Also, you can favorite any member of Congress and they'll appear under the favorites bar. You're homepage will also show the latest news articles about anyone you've favorited.

I messed up my search, how can I get back to the homepage?

No worries. Just click on "Congress Tracker" in the top left button of the navigation bar and it'll take you home. Or click here.
